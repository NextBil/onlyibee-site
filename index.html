ABOUT.TXT
readme_

ONLY IBEE — a creative dude. Born in Ivory Coast, grew up in Paris. Still mind-blown by just being alive and conscious — seriously, whaaat?

A versatile and curious artist who finds joy in both creation and exploration. With a passion for philosophy, I enjoy diving into deep thoughts and concepts — exploring the intersection of art and philosophy, committed to preserving the meaning and value of every piece.

When I'm feeling down, recording music turns me into the happiest person alive.

Human-made work filled with intention, connecting Paris, Toronto, and beyond — for those who seek something real and meaningful. This site is an engine for art and discovery. Scan a tag, enter the zone. Not everything is where you can see it.
