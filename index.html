ONLY IBEE

Only Ibee is a genre-blending artist and creative world — alternative RnB, soul and hip hop — built as a playable music console where every page reacts to the song that's playing.

The world

  • Music Universe — galaxies of releases, planets you can play
  • Lyrics Engine — synced karaoke lyrics and the story behind each song
  • Arcade — rhythm games that run on the music (Run Beebee, Mimi Neon Rush, Mimi Music Tiles)
  • ICS — Ibee's Chess Set characters: Beebee, Mimi, Goli, Swordy, Sworda, Nannan, Abla
  • UTOPIE — hand-made, NFC-tagged clothing, each piece unique
  • IBEE TV — the video channel
