WHY THESE NUMBERS ARE REAL_

NOT HAND-TYPED. Every number on this screen is read from the ONLY IBEE database when the page refreshes. The screen does not generate random numbers or add pretend activity.

VISITS. When someone enters the site, their browser gets an anonymous visitor token. It has no name, email or IP address. The same browser is counted once for monthly population, rather than once per page refresh.

ONLINE NOW. A visitor is shown as online only while their browser has checked in during the last two minutes. Leave the site and that signal naturally disappears.

SONG PLAYS. A play is saved only when the radio actually starts a song. Repeated taps or replays of the same song from the same browser within 30 minutes do not add extra plays. Each song's count in the radio list comes from those saved plays.

MEMBERS. CITIZENS is the real number of registered ONLY IBEE accounts. The public page can see only the final total — never account details.

The database is the source of truth. If it cannot be reached, this screen does not invent a replacement number.
